About Anime Campaign

Anime Campaign was a series of Twitch streams featuring JelloApocalypse and friends playing a tabletop role-playing game. They used a custom system (known both as the Anime Campaign System and the Epithets System), which was based on the URealms system. Most of the series was streamed on the Surprise Round Twitch channel (previously WarpedLamp).

There is also a scripted web series adaptation of Anime Campaign called Epithet Erased, which is availble on YouTube and VRV.
