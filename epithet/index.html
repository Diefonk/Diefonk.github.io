Epithet Index

Unofficial collection of Anime Campaign and Epithet Erased stuff

About

Anime Campaign was a series of Twitch streams featuring Brendan Blaber (JelloApocalypse) and friends playing a tabletop role-playing game. They used a custom system based on the URealms system. Most of the series was streamed on the Surprise Round Twitch channel.

Epithet Erased is a web series/light novel adaptation of Anime Campaign. You can find all of it on the official website.
